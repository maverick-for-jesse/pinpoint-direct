Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will my postcards go out?
Once you approve your design, we handle printing and hand off to USPS. Delivery typically takes 3–5 business days depending on your area.
Do I need to provide my own mailing list?
Nope! We build targeted mailing lists based on the zip codes, neighborhoods, or radius you specify. We can also target by homeowner status, home value, and more. If you already have a list, we can use that too.
What does the design process look like?
After you complete the onboarding wizard, our team creates postcard design options based on your business profile and campaign goals. You'll see a preview in your client portal and can request changes or approve it — all online, no back-and-forth emails needed.
What postcard sizes do you offer?
We offer standard 4×6 and oversized 6×9 postcards. Most clients prefer 6×9 for its higher visibility and better response rates.
Can I track my campaign?
Yes! Your client portal shows every campaign — status, estimated mail date, piece count, and more. You'll receive notifications as your campaign moves through each stage.
