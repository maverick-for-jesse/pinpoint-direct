Questions

Common Questions

How many postcards should I send per month?

We require a minimum of 200 postcards/month — that's $200/mo to start. Most clients begin there and scale to 300–500/month once they see results building. We'll help you figure out the right volume for your goals during onboarding.

Why is there a 200 postcard minimum?

Direct mail is a frequency game. Sending fewer than 200/month means too few people see your postcard often enough to take action — and clients who under-invest rarely see results. The 200-piece minimum isn't about revenue for us; it's about making sure every client we take on actually has a shot at winning.

What's the 3-month minimum about?

Direct mail is a repetition game. One mailer is rarely enough — the results compound over time as your name becomes familiar. We require 3 months because we've seen what happens when businesses quit too early: they leave the results on the table.

Where does the mailing list come from?

We build our own lists from official Coweta County property transfer records — updated monthly. You're reaching people who actually just bought a home, not recycled data from a broker who sold the same list to your competitors.

Can I cancel after 3 months?

Yes — after your 3-month commitment, you can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. Most clients choose to keep going once they see the results building.

What kinds of businesses is this best for?

Any local home services business with a solid job value — roofing, HVAC, concrete coatings, landscaping, pest control, cleaning, plumbing, and more. If new homeowners need your service and the job is worth $500+, direct mail to new movers makes sense.
